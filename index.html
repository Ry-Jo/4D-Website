What is Project Zephyr?

Project Zephyr is a three-dimensional storytelling website that tells the story of a dystopian city consumed by air pollution. People randomly explore the city or choose to be part of an active adventure. Project assets are built in Maya, imported into the WebGL framework three.js to create a 3D experience viewed through the Google Chrome browser.
